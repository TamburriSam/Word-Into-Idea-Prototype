Now for the creative part! Here are all your words, lined up in columns. Read each row of four words across. Do you see any striking connections and associations? Pick four or five rows that seem especially interesting to you and copy those 16 or 20 words onto the blank panel on the right.


Now write a poem using those words.


Pick another word from your lists to be the title. Then use the rest of the words to write your poem.

  • You can use as many other words as you like to make connections between the ones you chose.
  • You can change the forms of any of your words, make nouns plural, change the tense of the verbs.
  • The main thing is, don't worry about making sense: make poetry instead!

When you're done, post your poem to the group text at this link. You can do so anonymously or sign your work. Save the lists of all your words to this link. You'll do an exercise with them later.
