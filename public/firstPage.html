There are two ways to play this game

Solo

To play by yourself,
at your own pace,
select this button:


Solo


The game will set up as
if there are others
playing, but it's just you
and the AI.

Group

To play in a group,
in real time,
select this button:


Group


Select or create a room
and wait while the other
members join and you'll
be shown a starting screen.
